Configuring an SMTP Notification Publisher instance at PingFederate (for Gmail)



SMTP Server Settings Recap (for Gmail)

Email Server: smtp.gmail.com

SMTP Port: 587 (for STARTTLS) or 465 (for SSL/TLS)

Encryption Method: Choose STARTTLS or SSL/TLS based on your preference.

SMTPS Port: 465 (only needed if using SSL/TLS).

Username: Your Gmail email address.

Password: Get your App Password (https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords)
